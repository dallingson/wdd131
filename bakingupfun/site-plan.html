Overview

Purpose

Baking Up Fun is an online bakery that brings joy and sweetness to customers through handcrafted, high-quality baked goods. Our purpose is to provide fresh, delicious treats for a variety of customers.

Audience

Our primary audience includes individuals and families who love baked goods. It will also be designed for a company who wants to provide an easy shopping experience for their customers.

Dynamic elements

Responsive design that automatically adjusts layout and content sizing based on screen size (mobile, tablet, desktop). Interactive product search functionality allowing customers to find specific items by name or ingredient. Category filtering system to sort products by type (breads, pastries, cakes, etc.) with real-time results display.
